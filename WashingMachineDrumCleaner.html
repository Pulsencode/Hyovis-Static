How to use

FAQ

Follow these steps to use the Washing Machine Drum Cleaning Tablet:

  1. Put 2 tablets in the washing machine drum, not in the detergent box. Close the lid.
  2. Start the washing machine and choose the highest water fill level. Run for 5-10 minutes to dissolve the product completely.
  3. Skip any prewash. (For serious dirt, use 40-60 degrees warm water to enhance the active oxygen and decontamination performance.)
  4. Pause the machine and let it soak for 2-3 hours to allow the product to penetrate fully for the best decontamination effect.
  5. After soaking, resume the washing-rinsing-dehydration process.
  6. If there is more dirt, repeat step 4, then wipe off the dirt attached to the inner cylinder.
  7. It is recommended to use 4 tablets at a time.
  8. For regular cleaning, use 1-2 tablets at a time, depending on the machine's capacity.

Our product is suitable for all types of washing machines:

  • Front Load
  • Top Load
  • Semi Automatic
  • Fully Automatic
